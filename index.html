Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Agrarian Global?

An agrarian community without borders, whose collective prosperity is based on producing and maintaining crops and farmland. "GWD" is the amplifier of the "Return to Nature" agenda. A collection of organizations for the purposes of transforming lands back to a place of abundance.

What is the Return to Nature Agenda?

Mindfulness of the living world and recognition of the solutions under our feet. Harnessing economic empowerment through regenerative agriculture, while establishing repeatable models for current and future generations.

By showcasing all facets of what "We Do", we shall shine light on the lands, people and process.

Earth Care | People Care | Fair Share.

Where is Agrarian Global?

Across Africa, Indigenous Lands of Turtle Island.

How can I participate?

Connect with us here (link below), or on Social Media. There are various ways to participate, as there is alot going on and much to accomplish.

Capacity is our mission, vast lands sit idle.

How are you providing Local Food Security?

Empowering the indigenous peoples with tools and plan. Closed loop, Regenerative systems. Supply chain solutions.

West Africa has all that it needs to be Food Sovereign. By understanding subsistence farming and its challenges, we are able to help coordinate and drive network effects and efficiencies.

How are you providing Herbal Security?

Know your farmer. Alloting significant acreages to herbal cultivation, seed preservation. Working directly with indigenous herbal farmers. Import/Export Certifications and International Shipments.

How can you prove NON GMO / All Natural Organic?

Our Lands are Our Garden. Would you compromise your garden with chemicals or things made in a lab? We conduct ourselves with the uttermost transparency and accountability.

Organic is a new word. We work in-tune with Nature and are supported by world class individuals. Certifications via 3rd party organizations are not on our agenda at this time, cerifications coming soon.
